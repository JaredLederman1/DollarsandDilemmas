Meet Jared Lederman

Jared is a third year student at Cornell University studying Applied Economics & Management, originally from Marblehead, Massachusetts. At Cornell, he is involved with several finance and business organizations.

Jared has been interested in business and financial literacy since he was very young, founding a 3D printing business in elementary school and later a local newspaper. During summer break in college, he worked as a financial analyst intern and a venture capital intern. This coming summer, he'll be joining Susquehanna International Group.

Dollars & Dilemmas was established in 2025 when Jared recognized a severe gap in financial literacy education available to those at a middle school level. Jared recognized the importance of learning money skills at a young age, and wished throughout middle and high school that a course like D&D had existed. Gaining a strong foundation on top of which to build more complex habits is vital, and the earlier this process begins, the better.

If you have any questions about course content, enrollment, timing, or anything else, please feel free to reach out.
